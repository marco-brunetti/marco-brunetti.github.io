Cursed Silence: Christmas (2023)

Youtube Gameplays

I developed this game as a spinoff of Cursed Silence: Demo, building on its foundation. I completed it in 3 months, addressing the flaws I discovered in its predecessor. I decided to create a looping game level that depicts a mentally struggling person. The challenge was to create something concise before Christmas. I decided to scrap the idea of having an enemy and instead focus on a changing environment that creates horror and confusion for the player. I also made almost all objects interactable in some way, such as pickable items and others that triggered an inner monologue for the character.

Breakthroughs:

  • Lighting: My lighting and post-processing techniques improved significantly compared to the previous game, and I was able to better use the contrast between lights and shadows.
  • Level design: I designed a small level filled with things the player can interact with. I applied material modification and other techniques to achieve a concise visual character using third-party assets.
  • First-person controller: I improved the controller significantly compared to the previous game, making character movement and item interaction feel much more natural.

Drawbacks:

  • Storytelling: The greatest problem was that some players didn't understand the looping game mechanic and thought something had changed after the first gameplay. I must find a way to better communicate this aspect to the player.
